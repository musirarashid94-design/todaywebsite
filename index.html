WHERE TO GO!

Islamabad

Islamabad, the capital city of Pakistan is ranked as one of the most beautiful cities in the world.

Punjab

Punjab is Pakistan’s second largeat province by area, and it is most populated province, with rich culture and history.

Sindh

Sindh is the southeast of the country, and the historical home of the sindhi people. Sindh has Pakistan’s second largest economy, while its provincial capital, Karachi is Pakistan’s largest city and financial hub.

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa

Located in the nothwestern region of the country along the international border with Afghanistan.

Balochistan

It is the largest province in terms of area, forming the southwestern region of the country, but is the least populated. Its provincial capital and largest city id Quetta.
